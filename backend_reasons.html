Why Use Backend?

← Back to "How to make your website public"

Your website will need a backend if you want it to do any of the following things:

  • Have users
  • Use databases
  • Have the ability to shop online
  • Have the ability to connect to other people's apps and tools
  • Let users upload photos or videos, etc.
  • Do anything in real time
  • Track website visitors
  • Have visitor-generated content
  • Follow the law (there is certain user information you are legally required to keep private)
  • Have chatbots

I can help you set up a backend.

So can AI if you know what you're doing, but please double-check with me as you can get hacked or sued if you set up your backend wrong.
