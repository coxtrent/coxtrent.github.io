Synesthesia

Interactive audio-visual experience

C++, MIDI/realtime music, embedded software, Arduino framework, PlatformIO, ESP-32, circuitry; procedurally generated visuals driven by music;

Embedded program that runs on the ESP-32 microcontroller using the Arduino framework. Procedurally generates a creative LED light show in sync with the music using realtime MIDI-USB or audio-spectral data. The lights can also display custom animations.

My foray into this class of hardware marks my first exploration of embedded systems and microcontrollers, and deepened the understanding of circuitry and electronic principles I had already from music production.

LocalTrader

Grindr for Magic: The Gathering Players

Architected geospatial queries for finding nearby users, using PostGIS and Django ORM
